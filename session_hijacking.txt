When a victim loads this guestbook page, their browser reads this hidden script, looks up their active authentication cookies via document.cookie, and attempts to load an invisible image from an IP address controlled by the attacker (Port 9000), passing the cookie right in the URL parameter

Hijack the session (Browser B)
Open a completely separate browser application (like Chromium) or an Incognito Window in Firefox

Notice that it boots you out and forces you to the login screen because you aren't authenticated
Open your browser's Developer Tools (F12), head over to the Storage or Application tab, and locate Cookies
