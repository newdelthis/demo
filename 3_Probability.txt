Assignment 3
============
Question 1: Probability

Using the loan_approval_dataset.csv dataset, answer the following questions:

Let:
A = Applicant is a Graduate
B = Applicant is Self-Employed
C = Loan is Approved

Part A: Marginal (Unconditional) Probability
============================================
Calculate the following probabilities:

1. What is the probability that a randomly selected applicant is a Graduate?
2. What is the probability that a randomly selected applicant is Self-Employed?
3. What is the probability that a randomly selected applicant receives Loan Approval?

Part B: Joint (Unconditional) Probability
=========================================
Calculate the following probabilities:

4. What is the probability that an applicant is a Graduate and receives loan approval?
5. What is the probability that an applicant is Self-Employed and receives loan approval?
6. What is the probability that an applicant is both Graduate and Self-Employed?

Part C: Conditional Probability
===============================
Calculate the following probabilities:

7. What is the probability that an applicant receives loan approval given that the applicant is a Graduate?
8. What is the probability that an applicant receives loan approval given that the applicant is Self-Employed?
9. What is the probability that an applicant is a Graduate given that the loan was approved?
10. What is the probability that an applicant is Self-Employed given that the loan was approved?

Part D: Interpretation
======================
Based on your results:
11. Are Graduates more likely to receive loan approval than the overall applicant population? Justify using probabilities.
12. Are Self-Employed applicants more likely or less likely to receive loan approval compared to the overall population?
13. Which factor appears to have a stronger association with loan approval:
Education status, or
Self-employment status?

Support your answer using the conditional probabilities calculated above.
